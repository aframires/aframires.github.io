My main area of research is music and audio signal processing, together with machine learning, towards the development of interfaces to help electronic music production.

I'm currently a PhD student in the MTG,
where I'm conducting research on methods for incorporating large audio databases in electronic music production making.

Code

For my MSc thesis, I developed LVT, a Max for Live device which can be used to create rythmic phrases in Ableton Live through "beatboxing".

I'm developing Freesound Uploader, a VST plugin for uploading audio recordings to Freesound directly from your DAW.
